## Who should read what

### Site owner / WordPress administrator setting up the plugin for the first time
1. [Installation & Setup Guide](getting-started/installation-setup-guide.md) — read top to bottom before touching anything else.
2. [Troubleshooting & FAQ](troubleshooting/troubleshooting-and-faq.md) — refer to the "Installation issues" and "Settings issues" sections if anything does not work after setup.

### Developer maintaining or debugging the site
1. [Installation & Setup Guide](getting-started/installation-setup-guide.md) — skim the requirements table and the "What happens on activation" section so you know what the plugin creates automatically.
2. [Troubleshooting & FAQ](troubleshooting/troubleshooting-and-faq.md) — read in full; the Diagnostics section and the database/table checklist are most relevant.

### Hotel operations staff / front desk supervisor
1. [Troubleshooting & FAQ](troubleshooting/troubleshooting-and-faq.md) — focus on "Staff portal issues" and "Booking flow issues".
