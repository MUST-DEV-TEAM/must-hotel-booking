# Guest Booking Guide

Welcome! This guide walks you through booking your stay at the hotel using the online booking system — from searching for dates to receiving your confirmation.

---

## Step 1: Search for Availability

Look for the booking search form on the hotel website. It contains three fields:

1. **Check In Date** — the day you plan to arrive. Click the field and a calendar will appear. Select your arrival date.
2. **Check Out Date** — the day you plan to leave. The calendar will guide you to pick a valid departure date after your arrival date.
3. **Guests Number** — enter the total number of guests staying (adults and children combined). The minimum is 1. The maximum allowed is set by the hotel.

All three fields are required. Once you have filled them in, click the **Check Availability** button (or the arrow button next to it).

---

## Step 2: Browse and Select a Room

After searching, you will see a list of available accommodations that match your dates and guest count. Each card shows:

- A photo of the room (click any photo to open a larger view)
- The room name and a short description
- Up to four thumbnail images
- A **Book Now** button that takes you directly to checkout for that room
- An **Additional Details** link (if configured) that opens a page with more information about the room

Browse the options and click **Book Now** on the room you want.

If the search results page shows no rooms, the dates you selected may have no availability. Try different dates.

---

## Step 3: The Checkout Form (Guest Information)

After selecting a room you arrive at the **Guest Information** page. This is step 3 of 4 in the booking process.

### What information is required

The form collects the following details:

- **First name** — your given name
- **Last name** — your family name
- **Email address** — used to send your booking confirmation
- **Phone number** — including country dial code (click the flag/code to pick your country)
- **Country of Residence** — select from the dropdown

All of these fields are required before you can continue.

### Stay summary

On the same page you will see a summary of your stay:

- Accommodation name and rate plan (if the hotel uses rate plans)
- Arrival and departure dates
- Number of nights and number of guests
- Nightly rate, accommodation subtotal, any fees, any discounts, taxes, and the total price

### Coupon code

If you have a discount coupon code, enter it in the **Add Coupon** field at the top of the form and click the tick/apply button. The summary will update to show the discount. If the code is invalid or expired, a message will tell you.

When you are ready, click the **Next** button or proceed to the **Review and Payment** page.

---

## Step 4: Review and Payment

This is the final step before your booking is confirmed. You will review everything and choose how to pay.

### Payment options

The hotel may offer one or both of the following options:

**Pay Now (card / Stripe)**
- You pay the full amount (or the deposit amount, if a deposit is required) immediately using your credit or debit card.
- You will be redirected to a secure Stripe checkout page hosted by Stripe. Enter your card details there.
- After payment Stripe returns you to the hotel website where your booking confirmation is shown.
- Your payment is processed immediately. The booking is confirmed right away.

**Pay at Hotel**
- You do not pay anything now. Your booking is reserved and confirmed, but payment is collected when you arrive at the hotel.
- No card details are required for this option.
- Select this option and click Confirm to complete the booking.

### What is a deposit?

If the hotel has enabled a deposit requirement, you will be asked to pay a portion of the total price now rather than the full amount. The deposit can be a fixed amount or a percentage of the stay total. The remaining balance is settled at the hotel. *Unverified / needs confirmation: how and when the remainder is collected online is not shown in the guest-facing booking flow. Contact the hotel for details.*

### Confirm your booking

Once you have selected a payment method and reviewed all the details, click **Confirm reservation**. The system will process your selection:

- If paying by card, you are taken to Stripe's secure payment page.
- If paying at hotel, your booking is created immediately and the confirmation screen is shown.

---

## Step 5: Booking Confirmation

After your booking is completed you will see a confirmation screen on the hotel website. The screen will say:

- **"Your payment has been successful"** — if you paid by card online.
- **"Your booking has been successful"** with a note about hotel payment — if you chose pay at hotel.

### Confirmation email

A confirmation email is sent to the address you provided. The email contains:

- Your booking reference number
- Room name and stay dates
- Guest name
- Total price and payment method
- Hotel contact details (if configured)
- A link you can use to cancel the booking (see below)

If you do not receive the confirmation email within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder.

---

## Cancelling a Booking

Your confirmation email includes a **cancellation link**. Clicking that link takes you back to the booking confirmation page, which will process the cancellation automatically.

**Important conditions:**

- The cancellation link only works if your booking has not yet been completed, blocked, or already cancelled.
- **If you paid by card (Stripe):** the online cancellation link cannot automatically cancel your booking. You will see a message asking you to contact the hotel directly to discuss refund and cancellation options. A cancellation policy may apply.
- **If you chose pay at hotel:** the cancellation link will cancel the booking immediately as long as it has not been marked completed.
- A cancellation policy may apply depending on how close to your arrival date you cancel. If a policy is attached to your rate plan, the details are shown in any message you receive.

*Unverified / needs confirmation: there is no self-service cancellation page accessible from the website navigation. Cancellation is only available through the link in the confirmation email.*

---

## What to Do If Something Went Wrong

### No confirmation email received

1. Wait 5 minutes — delivery can sometimes be delayed.
2. Check your spam or junk mail folder.
3. Make sure you entered the correct email address. If you made a typo, contact the hotel and give them your name and the dates you booked.

### Payment failed or Stripe checkout was cancelled

If you left the Stripe payment page without completing payment, you will be returned to the hotel confirmation page with a message saying "Stripe checkout was canceled." Your booking has not been confirmed in this case. You can review the booking details and try again by selecting a payment method and clicking Confirm.

If your card was declined, try a different card or contact your bank.

### The dates or room are wrong

Contact the hotel directly as soon as possible. Staff can edit stay details and reassign rooms from the hotel's management system.

### The booking search shows no available rooms

Your selected dates may be fully booked, or the dates may not meet the hotel's minimum stay requirement. Try adjusting your dates or contact the hotel to ask about availability.
